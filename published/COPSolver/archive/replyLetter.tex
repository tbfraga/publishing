Dear editor and reviewer, \\

I greatly thank you for the improvement propositions for the paper "COPSolver: open source software for solving combinatorial optimization and other decision problems - library for solving the multi-product p-batch processing time maximization problem". \\

Next I present the modifications made in the paper:

\begin{itemize}
\item I made a slight modification in the title by exchanging "batch" for "p-batch", seeking to better adapt the name of the problem to the nomination adopted in the literature;
\item I modified the highlights to emphasize the impact of the paper;
\item I rewrote the abstract seeking to highlight the impact of the proposed software package and the new method;
\item I replaced the keyword "multi-product batch problem" with "p-batch problem";
\item In Section 2.1 Architecture Software, I replaced the word "folder" with "subfolder" when appropriate;
\item I changed the name of the section "Economic, technological, scientific and education impact" by "Impacts";
\item I rewritten the "Impacts" section by citing and referenced the impact of the method and software. I included a brief explanation of the p-batch problems referencing with a state of the art paper. I also explained the importance and application of the proposed method better;
\item In References section, I modified the first reference (paper name) and included two more references.
\end{itemize}


Coordially, 

Tatiana Balbi Fraga
